What is Ceramic Coating?

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer that chemically bonds to your paint, creating a hard protective layer that lasts years. Unlike wax which wears off in weeks, ceramic bonds at a molecular level and provides protection that endures.

The result repels water, dirt, and contamination - making every wash faster and easier and protecting your paint from UV fading, bird etchings, and light scratches for years.

After-Care Instructions

  • First 7 Days No washing for 7 days. Allow the coating to fully cure.
  • First 48 Hours Park in the shade for the first 48 hours.
  • Washing Protocol pH neutral shampoo only. H2O Coat at every wash maintains the coating.
  • Maintenance Annual inspection recommended to ensure longevity.

Note: All ceramic bookings include a complimentary paint condition inspection.
