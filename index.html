Ceramic Coating

The pinnacle of paint protection. We use industry-leading nano-ceramic formulations to armor your vehicle.

Why Ceramic Coating?

  • Unmatched Gloss The coating cures to form a glass-like layer, enhancing the depth and clarity of your paint beyond traditional waxes.
  • Hydrophobic Properties Water, mud, and grime bead up and slide off effortlessly, making maintenance washing incredibly easy and fast.
  • UV & Chemical Resistance Prevents paint fading from the harsh sun and protects the clear coat from bird droppings, tree sap, and acid rain.
  • Durability Unlike waxes that last weeks, our coatings are measured in years. We offer 3-year and 5-year protection tiers.
