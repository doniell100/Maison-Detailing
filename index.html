What is Ceramic Coating?

Ceramic coating is a professional-grade liquid polymer that chemically bonds to your vehicle's clear coat. Unlike wax, which sits on the surface and wears off within months, ceramic coating becomes part of the paint — and it stays there for years.

The result is a surface that water sheets off rather than sitting and spotting, where dirt cannot bond the way it normally would, and where UV rays, bird droppings, and environmental contamination have significantly less impact on the paint. Every wash becomes faster and easier. The gloss depth is noticeably richer than wax alone.

We use two Nasiol professional coatings depending on how long you want the protection to last. The Nasiol ZR53 delivers three years of protection in a single coat. The Nasiol NL272 — Nasiol's flagship product — delivers five years. Both are 10H hardness rated and applied only after full paint decontamination and surface preparation.

After-Care Instructions

  • First 7 Days No washing for 7 days. Allow the coating to fully cure.
  • First 48 Hours Park in the shade for the first 48 hours.
  • Washing Protocol pH neutral shampoo only. H2O Coat at every wash maintains the coating.
  • Maintenance We recommend our Maison Care Plan for coated vehicles to maintain the coating correctly with every wash. Annual inspection recommended to ensure longevity.
